Questions

The things people ask before they say yes.

Is it really free?

Yes. Live attendance is free. No card is needed to register. We ask only for your name and email so we can send the link and reminders.

Will it be recorded?

Yes. The recording is a separate, optional product so it can cover production and lifetime access. You will see it on the confirmation page after you register, and again after the event.

I have never done breathwork. Is this for me?

It is. There is nothing to learn before Sunday. The whole point is that the room is held for you. Anthony or Amy guides every breath, and trained facilitators are there throughout.

What if I cannot stay the full session?

Stay for as much as you can. The recording covers anything you miss, and we will send a short integration note in the days afterward.

What do I need to set up?

A quiet room, a mat or sofa to lie on, water, and headphones. Eat lightly an hour before. That is it.

What if my time zone does not match the session times?

You have two sessions to choose from, ten hours apart, so most time zones land at least one session at a workable hour. Both sessions are open to you with the same link.
