01
Remember

Reconnect with the body underneath the persona. Most of us are running an idea of ourselves. This is the part where the actual you reports back in.
02
Reframe

See the story you have been telling about your body, and the story your body has been telling you. Most of them were drawn before you were ten.
03
Resource

Build the inner capacity to hold what comes. We do not skip this stage. Without resourcing, deeper work re-traumatises rather than heals.
04
Regulate

Use breath as the override switch. This is the stage you will most clearly feel on Sunday. The body remembers it can rest.
05
Realign

Move forward with what is true now, not what was true at six. The choices that have felt impossible become obvious.
06
Rise

Live and lead from the regulated baseline you just built. This is what other people feel when they are around you.
