## Why this copy works

- **Opens on the reader's felt experience**, not the product. Earns attention before asking for it.
- **Names the real problem** (undertrained nervous system) without moralising or diagnosing.
- **"6 out of 10" line** creates immediate recognition for the high-functioning-but-not-thriving reader.
- **Specifics build trust**: exact times, Part 1 vs Part 2 distinction, what actually happens in the room.
- **"You do not observe. You breathe."** removes the biggest objection (is this just a talk?).
- **Three CTAs** at natural stopping points, never pushy. Each has a different micro-hook ("register free", "save your free spot", "register free").
- **Credibility is brief and earned**, not a bio dump.
- **Closing line** ("functioning fine but never quite feeling well") is the most specific pain point on the page and the last thing they read before deciding.
