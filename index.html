How it works

Map. Message. Meet.

A three-stage system designed around one outcome: warm conversations on your calendar. Nothing fancy — just a tighter loop than your competitors are running.

01 / MAP

Find buyers, not lists

We map your ICP signal-by-signal — funding, hiring spikes, tech stack, exec movement, intent data — so every account on the list has a reason to hear from you this quarter.

  • Triggers & intent layered onto firmographics
  • Verified contacts (≤2% bounce rate)
  • Account research briefs per target
02 / MESSAGE

Hooks that earn replies

Sharp, human openers grounded in a specific signal — not "saw you posted about leadership." We A/B test against real reply data, not vanity metrics, and kill what doesn't perform.

We run sequences, handle replies, qualify the conversation, and hand over warm meetings on your calendar. Your AEs show up to a buyer who already wants to talk.

FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

How is this different from a typical outbound agency?
Most agencies optimize for volume. We optimize for replies and meetings. We send less, target tighter, and put senior operators on every reply — so the meetings that hit your calendar are with buyers who actually want to talk.
Will you use AI to write our emails?
We use AI to research and personalize at the signal level — never to write generic openers. Every sequence is drafted by a human copywriter who has sent millions of cold emails. If a hook reads like ChatGPT wrote it, we kill it.
How fast can we see results?
First positive replies in week 2-3. First booked meetings in week 3-4. Pipeline rhythm by week 6. We share a 90-day roadmap before you sign so you know what good looks like at every checkpoint.
What about our domain reputation?
We never send from your primary domain. We provision and warm secondary sending domains, manage SPF/DKIM/DMARC, monitor deliverability daily, and rotate mailboxes to keep your reputation pristine.
Do we need an SDR team to work with you?
No. GoLeadSpark replaces or complements an SDR function. We run research, outreach, reply handling, and qualification end-to-end. Your AEs only see meetings that have already been qualified.
What happens after the 3-month minimum?
You go month-to-month. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. We've never had a client leave inside the first cycle — but the option is yours.
